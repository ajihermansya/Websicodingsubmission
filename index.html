Maternal and child monitoring application as an effort to detect early stunting

This application is designed to monitor the health of pregnant women and children with the aim of detecting early the risk of stunting. Stunting is a condition of growth failure in children under five years of age due to chronic malnutrition that occurs in the period of the first 1,000 days of life, starting from pregnancy until the age of two years of age.
